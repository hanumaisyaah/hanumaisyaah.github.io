My Recent Favourite Actor


Kim Seon-ho (Korean: 김선호; born May 8, 1986) is a South Korean actor under Salt Entertainment. He began his career performing in stage plays and made his television debut in 2017 with Good Manager. Since then, he has continued to star in television dramas, including Two Cops (2018), 100 Days My Prince (2018), Welcome to Waikiki 2 (2019), and Start-Up (2020). He made his variety show debut as cast member of 2 Days & 1 Night in 2019.
